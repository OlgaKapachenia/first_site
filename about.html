About me

My name is Ben Rock Wills. You can find me on Instagram at @SKETCMCR. I am from arty background as my mum has been in business for over 35 years doing fantastic original art. Growing up and watching her paint was incredible and even though I went in the career as a chef I always tried to find time to do a sketch or a drawing of a landscape or scene. As I am from South West of the UK, we have many different beautiful places to draw. The work you are seeing are sketches I have done around Manchester pubs and restaurants and some other.
